The Process

How to hire a transfer maid in 5 steps

We make the entire MOM transfer process seamless and stress-free — most clients complete it in 2–3 weeks.

01

Browse & Shortlist

Register a free verified account to view full biodata of available transfer helpers. (MOM regulations require login to view photos.) Shortlist your top matches.

02

Arrange an Interview

We coordinate with the helper (and her current employer) to schedule a face-to-face interview at our office, or a video call if preferred.

03

Confirmation & Documentation

Once you choose, we handle the service agreement and submit the Work Permit application via Singpass to MOM on your behalf.

When MOM grants the In-Principle Approval (IPA), we coordinate the official transfer date and arrange the new insurance & security bond.

We complete the 6-monthly medical (if due) and arrange the official handover to your home. Done — your new helper starts work.

FAQ

Everything you've been wondering

Can I hire a transfer maid whose Work Permit is expired?

No. A helper must have a valid Work Permit to be transferred. We can help you check the status. If her permit is cancelled, she will usually have to return to her home country first.

How many times can a maid transfer in Singapore?

There is no official MOM limit. However, frequent transfers in a short time can be a red flag — it may suggest she has trouble settling in. Always ask why she transferred from previous jobs.

Why do maids transfer?

Many positive reasons! The most common: previous employer moving overseas, children growing up, or simply completing a contract and wanting a new environment.

Does the new employer have to pay for the transfer?

Yes. The new employer is responsible for the costs — including MOM application fees, new insurance, security bond, and any agency fees.

What is a "Letter of Consent"?

It's a written, signed letter from the maid's current employer stating they agree to transfer her to a new employer. MOM requires this — we'll help you obtain it.

What if it doesn't work out?

We provide a replacement period. If things don't work out within the agreed time and reasons covered in your agreement, we'll help you find a new helper at no agency cost.
